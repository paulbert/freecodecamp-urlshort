URL Shortener Microservice

Example Creation:

https://fcc-paulbert-urlshort.herokuapp.com/new/http://www.google.com
https://fcc-paulbert-urlshort.herokuapp.com/new/https://www.amazon.com/s/ref=nb_sb_noss_2?url=search-alias%3Daps&field-keywords=javascript

Output:

{"original_url":"https://www.amazon.com/s/ref=nb_sb_noss_2?url=search-alias%3Daps&field-keywords=javascript","short_url":"https://fcc-paulbert-urlshort.herokuapp.com/ixtleoe2"}

This:

https://fcc-paulbert-urlshort.herokuapp.com/ixtleoe2

Will redirect to this:

https://www.amazon.com/s/ref=nb_sb_noss_2?url=search-alias%3Daps&field-keywords=javascript
